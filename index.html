Hack Reactor Application

Hi - my name is Brent Colson, and I am applying to get into the October Remote cohort. I was asked to build an application from scratch, which was a great opportunity to put together everything that I had learned in Code Academy, Treehouse, and Eloquent JavaScript.

I thought a fun project would be to create an application that would make it easy for people who are searching for a better pillow to find the perfect match. There is a company that I have purchased from in the past that makes AMAZING pillows, but their website (PillowsXpress.com) is pretty bad and it’s tough to find the right one. By asking the user a couple of questions about how they sleep and what their budget is, I could make a recommendation on which pillow they should purchase.
